Exit Intent

Exit Intent is a javascript library that is design to help you increase your landing page conversion rates.

Exit Intent enables you to display a modal before a user leaves your website, helping you increase your landing page conversion rates. According to some guys experience, you can expect a 7% to 15% conversion rate increase - depending on your audience, traffic quality and offer.

A powerful library

Exit Intent is incredibly easy to install and it comes with powerful features, helping you control every aspect of the user experience. Visit the GitHup repo to learn more!
